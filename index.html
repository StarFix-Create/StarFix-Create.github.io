Screen Sharing To Desktop Browser

Click the Button below to connect to your Desktop Browser.
this may take awhile. but once you do so...you can control/play your games
on your pc monitor,"FROM YOUR BROWSER" and use your keyboard as controls.
NOTE: Specifically made for io games since most io games have login problems from regualar web browser,
launching it from android fixs that problem. which is why this app was mainly made.
Another Tab will open With the app name, rendering it like a website. Therfore making it look a website.
